Turn the sphere into a donut — with your hands

  1. Scale: make a left fist (clutch), then raise / lower your right hand.
  2. Squash: open both hands → flatten the sphere into a disc.
  3. Bore: both open, pull apart → punch the hole through the middle.
  4. Smooth: both open, bring together → round it into a clean donut.

Keyboard fallback: [ ] scale · S squash · B bore · M smooth · R reset · O orbit
